RIDDLE ME THIS

Welcome to "Riddle Me This" puzzle game. It is designed to entertain and challenge your puzzle solving abilities. We hope you have fun trying to crack our riddles.

How to Play

To play the game, you must first register a username so that we can keep track of your progress. If you have already registered, then login and see if you can beat your score from last time. To complete the game and register a score on our scoreboard, you must answer all ten riddles. You can only move onto the next riddle once you have answered the current one correctly.

Scoring System

The scoring system is based on how many attempts it takes you to answer the riddle:

  • Attempt 1 = 9 points
  • Attempt 2 = 6 points
  • Attempt 3 = 3 points
  • Other Attempts = 1 point
